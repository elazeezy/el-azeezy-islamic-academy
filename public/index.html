About El-Azeezy Islamic Academy

El-Azeezy Islamic Academy is a calm, spiritually-focused online learning space dedicated to helping Muslims, especially Nigerian and African families across the UK, Europe, the Gulf, and around the world connect deeply with the Book of Allah and the knowledge of the Dīn.

We believe that learning Qur’an should be peaceful, structured, consistent, and built on good character (adab). Through personalised guidance, gentle instruction, and a clear curriculum, we help children, teens, and adults read and recite the Qur’an with confidence and beauty.

Our mission is simple: to help you and your family learn the Qur’an properly, love it deeply, and live by it with sincerity, discipline, and connection to Allah ﷻ.

Our Programs

Every student begins with a free assessment. After that, we create a personalised learning plan based on level, age, and goals. Below are the main programs we offer:

Qur’an Recitation & Tajwīd

Qur’an Recitation & Tajwīd

Learn correct pronunciation, makhārij, and tajwīd rules with guided recitation and weekly improvement feedback. Ideal for children, teens, and adults at any level, from complete beginners to advanced.

Hifz Program

Qur’an Memorization (Hifz Program)

A calm, consistent memorization system with structured daily/weekly guidance, strong tajwīd foundation, and regular revision sessions. For students who already read Qur’an fluently.

Arabic Language

Arabic Language

Learn to read, write, and understand Arabic with basic grammar, sentence structure, and Qur’anic vocabulary. Suitable for beginners to early intermediate learners.

Islamic Studies

Islamic Studies

Aqeedah, Fiqh, manners, purification, salah, daily rulings, Qur’an & Sunnah lessons — taught in a structured and age-appropriate way. For kids, teens, and adults.
